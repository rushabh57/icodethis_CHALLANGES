Features

Boards

Projects are represented as boards in the system, Along with displaying essential information about the project, they can be set to public or private, depending on the user's preferences

Members

to work together with the common goal of delivering a successful project. They can have several different roles as active members of the development or simply followers of the board.

Follow

Ahead offers the possibility to follow the development of a project and be notified of its activities. Users con follow entire boards, or they can choose to follow just specific tosks within a board..

Tasks

Tasks and subtasks are the smallest chunks of executable assignments, Tasks con be assigned to multiple users, given a priority and easily moved across different states in the development pipeline.

Timer

The time tracking feature enables the users to easily. measure the time spent on a specific task. Once tracked and logged, the time can be seen and edited in a dedicated Time Records view for additional control.

Recently Accessed

The boards that you are actively working on con be easily accessed with the Recently Accessed feature.
